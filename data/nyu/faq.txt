Q: How do I register for classes?
A: You can register for classes through the Yeshiva University student portal. Make sure to consult your advisor before finalizing your course schedule.

Q: What is the deadline to drop a course?
A: The deadline to drop a course without a grade penalty is usually within the first two weeks of the semester. Please check the academic calendar for exact dates.

Q: Where can I find the academic calendar?
A: The academic calendar is available on the New York University Registrar's website.
